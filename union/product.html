Matte stretch ceilings

The matte texture of the stretch ceilings is a universal solution for most interiors, as it has a classic type of perfectly painted ceilings. There are no reflections and glare on this surface.

Features of matte ceilings:
  • Do not reflect interior items
  • Huge selection of colors and textures of canvases
  • Visually increase the space
  • Durable canvas
  • Suitable for all rooms in the apartment
