3. Call the master to measure the room. Departure is free in Moscow and the Moscow region up to 50 km from the Moscow Ring Road. After the final choice, you will be given an estimate for stretch ceilings price for 1m2 with installation and operation. The exact amount of your order will be prescribed in the contract for the provision of services during registration. We guarantee a positive result.
