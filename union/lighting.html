Advantages of the magnetic track Slott for Technolight

Completed lighting

It is easy to move lamps, rearrange, extract and add new ones. The only restriction is the location of the tracks.

Magnetic fasteners

The magnets installed in the track allow you to independently design the lighting and move anywhere.

Modular concept

The basis of the system is a track, the length of the track can be any. Various LED lamps are installed in the track. Light sources and their position can be changed at any time.

Low blinding effect

The system of lighting scrapers in track systems is arranged so that it eliminates the appearance of glare on the surface. Soft multiple lighting is suitable for most rooms.

Slott for technolight magnetic track in a stretch ceiling

The track magnetic system is combined with linear lighting. Dampfer stretch ceilings are ordered in every third new building. This is explained by the safety and strength of the design. The track is provided with low-voltage nutrition. The voltage in the circuit is 48 V. To hold onto the lamps is not scary. The spots themselves work on LED lamps. LEDs consume a little energy, in the process of light production they practically do not heat up. If necessary, any element can be replaced in the system.

The Technolight system is friendly to a different design. Completed options for joining compounds with shallow profiles EuroKraab for PVC and Euroslott for fabric.

Profile material is a strong aluminum alloy that holds a stretch, while possessing a relatively lightweight. The system is mounted at four points. The strips are reliably fixed to the base basis.

The conductive tires themselves are located vertically, therefore, they are not visible even with the lights directed at them. All components are invisible to the eye.
