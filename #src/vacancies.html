Training of installation brigades

Our company is continuously expanding. The number of orders increases annually. As a result, the staff of employees is increasing.Installers are key workers - a link between the organization and the customer. Accordingly, each master should be able to work with all ceiling structures and show an impeccable result.

How everything happens:
  • 1. We process all the responses to the vacancy.
  • 2. We call and invite you to an introductory lecture, where the basic principles of work are discussed and the ability of each applicant to fulfill the upcoming tasks is evaluated.
  • 3. After selection, all newcomers are sent to training warehouses.
  • 4. Then we move on to practice. We attach each student to the installation team for a week for visual consolidation of theoretical knowledge.
  • 5. The final stage is an exam at which each internships a test for knowledge of the theory and demonstrates the experience gained.
  • 6. When successfully passing the exam, we accept the student in the state.
