Answers to frequently asked questions about stretch ceilings

Content
1. How to choose PVC ceiling or fabric?

  • 1. How to choose PVC ceiling or fabric?

    Choose PVC if you need a sealed surface with protection against leakage. Give preference to fabric, if you need to make a ceiling that passes the air, maintains its circulation inside the structure.

  • 2. Matte, glossy or satin what to install?

    Matte is suitable for any interior, the surface is smooth, slightly rough to the touch. Glossy reflects light, conveys the outline of interior items. It is suitable for rooms, in which you need to add light, space. Satin transfers glare from light, shimmers like a mother of pearl. Which is more suitable, choose.

  • 3. How long will stretch ceilings drop?

    Basically, the height is reduced by only 4 cm. The type of profile, available communications, built - in lighting affects the departure.

  • 4. What is the wallpaper first (painting walls) or stretch ceilings?

    Option 1: first make the ceiling, then paint the walls, glue the ceiling skirting boat from polyurethane or foam.

    Option 2: First, finish the repair of walls, floor, and then carry out installation of stretch ceilings.

  • 5. How long will it take to apply to the start of installation?

    At least 24 hours, that is, the next day.

  • 6. How long will the installation of a stretch ceiling take?

    The execution time depends on the volume and complexity of the order. On average, 10m2 installation takes 1.5-2 hours.

  • 7. How to care for stretch ceilings?

    PVC film can be wiped with a damp cloth, and then wipe dry dry. You can not wash the fabric canvas, you can remove the pollution with a locally dry foam.

  • 8. The neighbors flooded, what to do?

    PVC holds water weighing up to 100 liters. To drain the water bubble, it is necessary to bring the liquid to any hole: a lamp, a ventilation grill, a chandelier. If there are no holes, then the water is drained through a partially removed canvas.

  • 9. It is possible to install if the air conditioner is installed?

    No, if the minimum distance to the ceiling is at least 5-7 cm. If less, then the air conditioner needs to be lowered.

  • 10. What will be the wizard, can he conclude an agreement?

    Yes, in measurement they will be shown samples of materials, they will talk about current promotions with gifts, discounts. After agreeing on the design and selection of material, you will be offered to conclude an agreement with the final cost of the service.
