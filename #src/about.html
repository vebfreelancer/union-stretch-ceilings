Pay for the order

To pay (input of the details of your card) you will be redirected to the payment gateway of Oshchadnyi bank. The connection with the payment gateway and the transfer of information is carried out in a protected mode using the SSL encryption protocol. If your bank supports the technology of safe conducting Internet payments Verified by Visa, MasterCard Securecode, Mir Accept, J-Secure, a special password may also be required to make a payment. This site supports 256-bit encryption. The confidentiality of the reported personal information is ensured by Oshchadnyi bank. The entered information will not be provided to third parties with the exception of cases provided for by the legislation of the Russian Federation. Bank cards payments are carried out in strict accordance with the requirements of payment systems of the world, Visa Int., MasterCard Europe SPRL, JCB

For owners of MAESTRO cards: Make sure your card does not have an inscription "The card is not intended for payments on the Internet." In this case, payment is most likely to be successful. If this inscription is not, but the payment still does not pass, most likely the possibility of paying for purchases on the Internet is disabled on your card. You can find out more about connecting this service in the issuing bank.
